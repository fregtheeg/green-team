Five Steps to Bystander Action:

  1. Decide "in your gut" that something is unacceptable. Many times, victims may not be in a position to explicitly ask for help.
  2. Ask yourself, "If no one intervenes, what will happen?" One of the biggest obstacles to intervening is believing that someone else will step in. This often reults in no one stepping in.
  3. Determine risks for taking action and ask, "Is there a low-risk option? How could I reduce risks to myself and others?"
  4. Check the "Notifications" page to see if the target has sent out a help message. Keep in mind that s/he may not have this app or the time to send a message.
